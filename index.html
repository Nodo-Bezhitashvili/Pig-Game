The Pig Game is a two-player dice game. Players take turns rolling a virtual six-sided die by clicking the "Roll Dice" button. The goal is to accumulate points, but if a player rolls a 1, their current score is lost, and it becomes the next player's turn.Players can choose to "Hold" their current score, adding it to their total. The first player to reach or exceed 20 points wins the game. A "New Game" button is provided to reset scores and start a new match.The interface displays each player's total score, current score, and whose turn it is. I hope you enjoy this game. Good luck!
